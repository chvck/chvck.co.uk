Boars Hill Ride

Posted on 07 Apr 2013

Another lovely day today so I decided to go out on the MTB as planned. I was going to be riding alone so couldn't really be bothered with driving anywhere - that left me with Boars Hill. It was pretty bone dry and really fast. Not a very demanding route and a fair bit on road but a couple of short, steep climbs and one descent that's actually pretty good. It's just off the main path and is actually pretty technical and hard to go fast on. There are also some impressively massive houses up the top of boars hill and as I passed one there were 2 Ferrari's waiting to pull out.

Managed about 20km and took just over an hour, pretty fun little ride and ideal for after work! This was also the first ride in aaaages where I've taken no bag so no tools, no water etc... Felt quite nice not having the bag on my back!
