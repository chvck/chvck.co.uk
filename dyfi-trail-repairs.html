Dyfi Trail Repairs

Sun 29 April 2012
By chuck

In Riding.

tags: bikesridingtrails

Today Dan, Gaz and I met up with the some of the locals to do some repairs to a couple of the Dyfi enduro descents. We split into 2 groups and our group went off to the top of the world cup descent to fill in a bit of a rut. The reasoning behind this was that the 2 jumps near the beginning of the trail have been built up a bit and the only real line leads straight into them! As such we filled in a bit of a massive rut to provide a nice line that leads around the jumps. Think I'll have to hit the jumps next Sunday, will be severely punished if I come up short though!

After filling in this rut we moved over to a piece of trail that I've not seen before that will be used as a climb. It's fairly, steep and loose-ish, no surprises there. About midway up we met the second group clearing back some brash and draining a massive puddle. Nothing much to do so we headed back down again. I get a feeling that I will be off and pushing for this climb, it's hard!

Next up we headed to 3in1 to cut in a bit of a new line at the top to get round the huge puddle there. Lots of raking and general moving of bits and pieces and there is now a nice line that should be quite good to ride over.

Oh, also - the weather was utterly, utterly gopping! Howling wind and tipping down for the most part. Dyfi Enduro is going to be weeeeet!
