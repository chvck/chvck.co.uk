Photo a day 8th Jan

Posted on 08 Jan 2013

Yesterday my new mtb light arrived at the office and I picked it up today, given that it had an expected delivery date of 2nd Feb I was pretty surprised! It cost me Â£20 from amazon. At 20 quid I wasn't sure what to expect but 2 of my mates have the same unit and have had it for quite a while so I confident that it was a good gamble. I was expecting it to be plastic and a bit rattley but it's actually metal and feels nicely solid. The battery pack hasn't convinced me that it'll hold up to any serious rain but as i plan on throwing that in my backpack anyway it isn't a big deal. Seems like it should be plenty bright enough - the case claims 1200 lumens but I know that's not gonna happen. I'm fairly certain it's a cree T6 LED which has a theoretical maximum of 1200 lumens but I've also seen lab results of tests on it, in ideal conditions in the lab they don't really even reach 1000 lumens! None-the-less it looks like a good little unit and I can't wait to get out and use it!
