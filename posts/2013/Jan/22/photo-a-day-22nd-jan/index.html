Photo a day 22nd Jan

Posted on 22 Jan 2013

New frame! For a while now I've been keeping a loose look out for a full suspension bike somewhere between 140mm and 160mm in travel. For a while I've had my eye on the Tomac Vanish but really couldn't find anywhere to buy in the UK so I moved onto the latest model of the Cove Gspot which CRC have at £1150 or £999 for the 2010 model. This was quite sorely tempting but a little more than I wanted to spend so I was still on the fence about it. Then I stumbled across an Intense Tracer 2 on STW for £700 that looked to be in really good condition and looked well looked after too. After a few brief questions I bought it and it arrived today. It's a great looking bit of kit and is in really good nick!
