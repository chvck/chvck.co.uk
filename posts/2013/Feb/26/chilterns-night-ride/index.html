Chilterns Night Ride

Posted on 26 Feb 2013

I've recently got back in touch with a guy called Roger who I emailed when we first moved to Oxford about riding. He's part of a riding group who ride areas around Oxford, he passed me onto another guy called Ainsley. They had a ride planned for Tuesday riding in the Chilterns starting from Watlington, only downside being that they were starting at 6pm. It's about 18 miles away but involves going on the Oxford ring road - to be there for 6 that also meant going during rush hour. No matter I said that I'd be and started work early so that I could leave the house at about 5.15pm, I got a bit lose in Watlington trying to find the car park so got there about 6.05. Turned out that there were only 3 of us; Ainsley, Matt and myself.

We did introductions, setup bikes and headed out. To begin with we had a fairly long, decent gradient climb. This had me suffering, it takes me a good while to get warmed and this just wasn't the ideal way to do it! I had to take a couple of stops on the way up but wound up at the top, not too long a climb really. There weren't any really bad climbs on the ride, there were a few really muddy bits but not too many although the ground in places was quite draggy anyway. We did a couple of pretty cracking, fun descents. Nothing technical this time but i'm assured that there is plenty of steep, technical stuff in the area.

Toward the end of the ride there was a small set of steps that I managed to totally OTB down, I started without any run in and there was a bit of a depression before the wooden holdy bit in the step so I wound up doing my best superman impression. After that were a couple of steep little ladder things that were pretty good fun though. About a mile before the end my light just completely cut out with now warning which wasn't so good so I need to investigate if there's a loose connection or something. I think that I'll also buy a spare battery pack as they're so cheap.

All in all a really fun ride and I'm really glad that I got out there, hoping for more rides with these guys to come!
