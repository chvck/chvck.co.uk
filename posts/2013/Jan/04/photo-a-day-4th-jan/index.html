Photo a day 4th Jan

Posted on 04 Jan 2013

Todays photo is something that I am rather fond of - food and cooking. I was pretty desperate for ideas so loaded up all recipes and whacked "sausages" and "squash" into the recipe finder. To my surprise there were actually results, I settled on a recipe of Chef John's Stuffed Summer Squash. I had to make a couple of alterations, cheddar for goats cheese, butternut squash and I also added mushrooms and a couple of chillies; I also decided to cook up some pasta to have with it. Oh, I also have no idea what Merguez sausages are so I just used standard ones. As I had some stuffing left after filling the squashes I mixed it into the cooked pasta.
