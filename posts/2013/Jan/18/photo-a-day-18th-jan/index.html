Photo a day 18th Jan

Posted on 18 Jan 2013

I was so determined not to take a snowy picture today but, well, that failed! This is overlooking the small close that we live in and was taken at about lunch time. There's a good few inches and I'm glad that I didn't bother driving into work today. Ben was due to come over for the weekend but has decided to postpone until the morning to assess the roads then. The Chilterns ride with Beeline Cycles has also been cancelled. Hopefully we'll get a short loop in locally at ssome point over the weekend.
